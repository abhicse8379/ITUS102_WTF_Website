How each component works

INTERNAL · 10 MARKS

Continuous Practical Evaluation

  • All 12 practicals are assigned and assessed week-wise by the lab faculty.
  • Students prepare one Performa document per practical, with screenshots of completed tasks, submitted as an assignment.
  • Faculty grades each out of 10; the 12 scores are aggregated into a final mark out of 10 for the OBE sheet.
INTERNAL · 10 MARKS

Internal Practical Exam

  • Run after 6–8 weeks of lab sessions, once roughly two units are complete.
  • Conducted in a proctored environment with a safe exam browser.
  • 4 practical sets, arranged in even/odd mode across both divisions, covering HTML5, CSS, DOM, Git, and JavaScript (including regex and secure input validation) — 30 marks, mapped down to 10.
INTERNAL · 5 MARKS

Mini Project Demonstration

  • Evaluated after week 13, at submission time.
  • Each student gets an individual mini-project statement to build as a full-stack application.
  • Graded out of 20 across front-end, back-end, and database connectivity, then mapped to 5.
EXTERNAL · 20 MARKS

External Practical Examination

  • Conducted by the university at the end of the semester in a proctored, safe-exam-browser environment.
  • Three practical task variants per batch, each evaluated on front-end, back-end, and database connectivity.
  • Full 20 marks carried through as the final external practical score.
EXTERNAL · 5 MARKS

Viva-Voce

  • Conducted at semester end by invited external experts.
  • Each student is evaluated individually out of 5.
